Built-in Pipes Examples

Original Text (Without Pipes) :

(currentDate) => {{currentDate}}

Pipe

(currentDate | date) => {{currentDate | date}}

Parameterized Pipe

(currentDate | date: "fullDate") => {{currentDate | date: "fullDate"}}

(currentDate | date: "dd/MM/yy") => {{currentDate | date: "dd/MM/yy"}}

(currentDate | date: "mediumTime") => {{currentDate | date: "mediumTime"}}

Multiple Parameterized Pipe

(currentDate | date: "mediumTime" : "UTC") => {{currentDate | date: "mediumTime" : "UTC"}}

(currentDate | date: "mediumTime" : "GTM) => {{currentDate | date: "mediumTime" : "GTM"}}

Chained Pipe

(currentDate | date: "fullDate" | uppercase) => {{currentDate | date: "fullDate" | uppercase}}

(currentDate | date: "fullDate" | lowercase) => {{currentDate | date: "fullDate" | lowercase}}
